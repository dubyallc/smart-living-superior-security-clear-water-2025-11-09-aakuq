About Duby LLC

Learn about our mission to transform modern living

Founded in 2015, Duby LLC emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create comprehensive home solutions that seamlessly integrate automation, security, and wellness technology. Our founders, recognizing the fragmented nature of home improvement services, set out to build an ecosystem where cutting-edge technology meets intuitive design. Starting as a small team of engineers and designers passionate about innovation, we've grown into a trusted partner for thousands of homeowners who demand more from their living spaces. Beyond products and services, we're dedicated to building a community of like-minded individuals who value their homes, their security, and their wellbeing. Our systems are designed with redundancy and backup power in mind. All critical security components include battery backup that keeps your system operational for 24-48 hours during a power outage. The security system will continue monitoring and can send alerts to your phone if any unusual activity is detected. Once power is restored, your system automatically resumes full operation without requiring manual intervention. We also recommend having a backup internet connection (like a mobile hotspot) for continuous remote access to your systems during extended outages.

Filter replacement intervals depend on your water quality and usage patterns. Typically, pre-filters require replacement every 3-6 months, while the main filtration cartridge lasts 6-12 months. We also provide guidance on which filters are best for your specific water conditions.
